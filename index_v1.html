/ Our Story

Born from Obsession

GeoDor Fashion was founded on a single belief: clothing is the language of self.

We build each collection around the tension between structure and freedom — geometric precision meeting organic movement. Every piece is an argument against mediocrity.

From our atelier, we collaborate with artisans across three continents to source materials that are as responsible as they are beautiful.
